Personal Action Plan

Water conservation starts with individual responsibility. This page outlines my goals, challenges and actions that I will take to reduce water consumption.

🎯 My Goal

Reduce my day-to-day water usage by 10%-15%. In the long term, I plan to install water-efficient technologies to further improve conservation.

⚠ Personal Challenge

One habit I struggle with is leaving the tap running while doing other tasks. This can waste more water than I realise.

Actions I Will Take

  • Turn off taps when not actively using water.
  • Take shorter showers.
  • Monitor water usage regularly.
  • Avoid unnecessary water waste.
  • Use water-efficient appliances where possible.

Tips For Others

  • Be aware of your daily habits.
  • Keep track of water consumption.
  • Set realistic conservation goals.
  • Review your progress regularly.

Reflection

Through researching water conservation, I realised that many everyday habits contribute to unnecessary water waste. By making small changes in my daily life, I can contribute to a more sustainable future.
